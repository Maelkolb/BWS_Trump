Best-Worst-Scaling Valence Annotation

Task Description:
You will see sets of four words extracted from a the Inaugural Address of President Trump. Your Task is to judge the sentiment of the words and annotate the two terms conveying the most postive (best)and most negative (worst) sentiment.

Choose the term you find most positive (Best) and most negative (Worst).
